Daily Reflection

Getting to know yourself better is a great way to gain insights that both you and people around you will benefit from!

This is an opportunity for you to take a few minutes off from the buzz around you and reflect inwards.

You choose the length yourself - it may be one minute or several, depending on where you are at today.

Note: No data will be saved or collected from this app. It is meant for personal use.

Slack reminders

Follow these examples to create and manage reminders using Slack.

Personal reminder

Remind a channel

/remind #name-of-channel Remember to reflect every weekday at 15.30

 

Update or delete reminders

To update, delete and re-create the reminder. To delete reminders and delete from there. /remind list

Note: modify the date and time as you see fit. E.g: on Fridays at 9 AM, every day at 14.45.
